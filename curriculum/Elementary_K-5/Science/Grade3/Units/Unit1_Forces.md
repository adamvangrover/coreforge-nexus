# Unit 1: Forces and Interactions

## Overview and Alignment
**NYSSLS Focus:** 3-PS2-1, 3-PS2-2, 3-PS2-3, 3-PS2-4
This unit explores the effects of balanced and unbalanced forces on the motion of objects and the patterns of an objectâ€™s motion. Students will also investigate electric and magnetic interactions between objects not in contact.

## Core Concepts
1.  **Balanced and Unbalanced Forces:** Understanding how forces affect motion.
2.  **Patterns of Motion:** Predicting future motion based on observed patterns.
3.  **Electric and Magnetic Forces:** Non-contact forces and their applications.

## Instructional Strategies & Resources
*   **Hands-on Investigations:** Students will use toy cars, magnets, and pendulums to observe forces.
*   **Engineering Design:** Design a simple problem-solving device using magnets (e.g., a magnetic latch).
*   **Phenomenon-Based Learning:** "Why does the swing keep going?" and "How do maglev trains work?"

## Assessment Strategies
*   **Formative:** Observation of student investigations, science notebook entries on force diagrams.
*   **Summative:** Performance task: Design a magnetic game or solution.
*   **Checklist:** Can the student explain cause and effect relationships in motion?

## Resources
*   Magnets (bar, horseshoe, ring)
*   Toy cars, ramps, weights
*   Spring scales
