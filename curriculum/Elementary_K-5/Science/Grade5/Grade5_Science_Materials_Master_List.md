# Grade 5 Science: Master Materials List

This list aggregates all materials required for the CoreForge Nexus Grade 5 Science curriculum, organized by Unit and Investigation.

## Unit 1: Structure and Properties of Matter
**Mandated Lab: What's in the Bag?**
*   [ ] Baking Soda (Sodium Bicarbonate) - 5 lbs
*   [ ] Calcium Chloride (Road Salt/Ice Melt) - 2 lbs
*   [ ] Phenol Red Solution (or Cabbage Juice) - 500 mL
*   [ ] Ziplock Sandwich Bags (Heavy Duty) - 1 box/class
*   [ ] Medicine Cups (Plastic) - 1 sleeve
*   [ ] Plastic Spoons
*   [ ] **Safety Goggles** (1 per student) - *Critical*

**Lesson Materials:**
*   [ ] Balloons (Lesson 1)
*   [ ] Digital Scales (0.1g precision) - 6 per class
*   [ ] Syringes (Needle-less) - 1 per student
*   [ ] Salt, Sugar, Gravel (for mixing)
*   [ ] Beakers/Clear Cups

## Unit 2: Matter and Energy in Ecosystems
**Mandated Lab: Circle of Life**
*   [ ] Organisms: Mealworms (Larvae) OR Wisconsin Fast Plant Seeds
*   [ ] Habitat containers (Plastic cups or terrariums)
*   [ ] Bran/Oatmeal (Bedding for mealworms)
*   [ ] Potting Soil (for plants)
*   [ ] Grow Lights (if no sunny window)
*   [ ] Magnifying Glasses

**Lesson Materials:**
*   [ ] Yarn (Lesson 17 Food Web)
*   [ ] Card stock (for organism cards)
*   [ ] 2-Liter Bottles (for Capstone Eco-Columns)
