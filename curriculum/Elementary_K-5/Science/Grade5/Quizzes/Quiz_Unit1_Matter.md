## Answer Key (Teacher Use Only)

1. **b)** Anything that has mass and takes up space.
2. **c)** Solid
3. **c)** Solubility
4. **c)** Rust forming on a bike
5. **Answer should mention:** Air is made of gas particles. When blown into the balloon, these particles move around rapidly and collide with the inner walls of the balloon, pushing it outward and causing it to expand.
6. **Possible answers:** Color, hardness, magnetism, electrical conductivity, reflectivity.
