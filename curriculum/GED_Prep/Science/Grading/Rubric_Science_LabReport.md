# Qualitative Assessment: Science Lab Report Rubric

| Criteria | 4 - Excellent | 3 - Proficient | 2 - Basic | 1 - Below Basic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hypothesis** | Clear, testable prediction with scientific reasoning ("If... then... because..."). | Testable prediction with some reasoning. | Prediction is unclear or lacks reasoning. | Missing or irrelevant. |
| **Data Collection** | Data is recorded accurately in neat tables with units. | Data is recorded but may lack units or neatness. | Data is incomplete or disorganized. | Significant data missing. |
| **Analysis** | detailed analysis of trends; correctly identifies outliers or sources of error. | Describes trends; attempts to identify error. | Simply restates data without analysis. | Missing analysis. |
| **Conclusion** | Conclusion directly answers the hypothesis using data as evidence; connects to scientific concept. | Conclusion answers hypothesis with some evidence. | Conclusion does not use data or fails to address hypothesis. | Missing conclusion. |
| **Safety & Participation** | Followed all safety rules; actively participated and cleaned up. | Followed safety rules; participated. | Needed reminders for safety or participation. | Unsafe behavior or lack of participation. |
