# GED Social Studies Extended Response Rubric

This rubric is used to score essay responses for tests and assignments.

| Criteria | 4 - Excellent | 3 - Proficient | 2 - Basic | 1 - Below Basic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Creation of Arguments and Use of Evidence** | Argument is clearly stated, complex, and well-supported by specific evidence from the text/knowledge. | Argument is stated and supported by evidence, though some points may be general. | Argument is present but weak; evidence is vague or insufficient. | No clear argument; evidence is missing or irrelevant. |
| **Development of Ideas and Organizational Structure** | Ideas are logically sequenced; transitions are effective; conclusion is strong. | Ideas are organized; some transitions used; conclusion is present. | Organization is choppy; ideas are random; no conclusion. | No organization; difficult to follow. |
| **Clarity and Command of Standard English** | Excellent grammar, usage, and mechanics; precise vocabulary. | Good control of grammar; minor errors do not interfere with meaning. | Frequent errors in grammar/mechanics that distract the reader. | Severe errors make the response unintelligible. |

**Total Score**: /12
