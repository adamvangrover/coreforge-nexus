# Quiz Unit 1: Civics and Government

**Instructions**: Answer the following questions.

1.  **Multiple Choice**: The system of checks and balances is designed to:
    a) Make the government run faster.
    b) Prevent any one branch from becoming too powerful.
    c) Ensure the President has total control.
    d) Eliminate the need for courts.

2.  **Multiple Choice**: Which branch of government is responsible for interpreting laws?
    a) Legislative
    b) Executive
    c) Judicial
    d) Federal

3.  **Short Answer**: Explain the difference between a "Right" and a "Responsibility" of citizenship. Give one example of each.

4.  **Matching**: Match the document to its description.
    *   A. Declaration of Independence
    *   B. Constitution
    *   C. Bill of Rights

    1.  The supreme law of the land establishing the government framework. (___)
    2.  The first ten amendments protecting individual liberties. (___)
    3.  Declared the colonies free from British rule. (___)

5.  **Critical Thinking**: Why is freedom of the press important in a democracy?

---
**Answer Key**
1.  b
2.  c
3.  Rights are freedoms protected by law (e.g., speech); Responsibilities are duties citizens should perform (e.g., voting).
4.  1-B, 2-C, 3-A
5.  It holds the government accountable and keeps citizens informed.
