# Quiz: Unit 1 - Kinematics Basics

## Instructions
Answer all questions. Show your work for calculation problems.

## Part 1: Multiple Choice (5 points each)

1.  **Which of the following is a vector quantity?**
    a) Speed
    b) Distance
    c) Mass
    d) Displacement

2.  **A car travels 100 meters East and then 100 meters West. What is its displacement?**
    a) 200 m
    b) 100 m East
    c) 0 m
    d) 100 m West

3.  **The slope of a position-time graph represents:**
    a) Acceleration
    b) Displacement
    c) Velocity
    d) Distance

## Part 2: Problem Solving (10 points each)

4.  **A runner accelerates from rest to a velocity of 8 m/s in 4 seconds. Calculate the acceleration.**

5.  **Using the result from Q4, how far did the runner travel in those 4 seconds?**

---
## Answer Key

1.  **d) Displacement**
2.  **c) 0 m**
3.  **c) Velocity**
