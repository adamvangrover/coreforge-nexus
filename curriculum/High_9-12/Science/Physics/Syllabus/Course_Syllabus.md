# Physics Course Syllabus Planner

## Course Information
*   **Course:** High School Physics (Regents/Honors)
*   **Prerequisites:** Algebra I and Geometry (Concurrent enrollment in Algebra II recommended)
*   **Term:** Full Year (40 Weeks)
*   **Instructor:** [Teacher Name]

## Pacing Guide & Weekly Schedule

### Quarter 1: Mechanics - Kinematics and Dynamics

*   **Week 1: Introduction to Physics & Mathematical Tools**
    *   Topics: Scientific notation, SI units, dimensional analysis, significant figures.
    *   *Lab/Activity:* Measurement and Uncertainty.
    *   *Resources:* Textbook Ch. 1.

*   **Week 2: Kinematics - 1D Motion (Scalars & Vectors)**
    *   Topics: Distance vs. Displacement, Speed vs. Velocity, Scalars vs. Vectors.
    *   *Lesson:* [Physics_Lesson2_Scalars_Vectors](../Lessons/Unit1/Physics_Lesson2_Scalars_Vectors.md)
    *   *Lab/Activity:* Walking the Graph (Motion sensors).

*   **Week 3: Kinematics - 1D Motion (Acceleration)**
    *   Topics: Average and Instantaneous Acceleration, Kinematic Equations.
    *   *Lesson:* [Physics_Lesson1_Intro_Kinematics](../Lessons/Unit1/Physics_Lesson1_Intro_Kinematics.md)
    *   *Lab/Activity:* [Experiment_01_Motion_Graphing](../Experiments/Experiment_01_Motion_Graphing.md)

*   **Week 4: Kinematics - Graphical Analysis**
    *   Topics: x-t, v-t, a-t graphs, area under the curve.
    *   *Assessment:* [Quiz_Unit1_Kinematics](../Quizzes/Quiz_Unit1_Kinematics.md)

*   **Week 5: Vectors and 2D Motion**
    *   Topics: Vector addition/subtraction, Components, Projectile Motion basics.
    *   *Demonstration:* [Demo_01_Velocity_Vectors](../Demonstrations/Demo_01_Velocity_Vectors.md)

*   **Week 6: Projectile Motion Advanced**
    *   Topics: Horizontally launched and Angled projectiles.
    *   *Lab/Activity:* Projectile Launcher Lab.

*   **Week 7: Review and Unit 1 Assessment**
    *   *Review:* Unit 1 Review.
    *   *Assessment:* [Test_Unit1_Kinematics](../Tests/Test_Unit1_Kinematics.md)

*   **Week 8: Dynamics - Newton's Laws I & II**
    *   Topics: Force, Mass, Inertia, F=ma.
    *   *Lab/Activity:* Inertia Demonstrations.

*   **Week 9: Dynamics - Newton's Law III & Friction**
    *   Topics: Action-Reaction, Static and Kinetic Friction.
    *   *Lab/Activity:* Coefficient of Friction Lab.

*   **Week 10: Dynamics - Applications**
    *   Topics: Ramps, Tension, Systems of masses.
    *   *Assessment:* Quarter 1 Exam.

### Quarter 2: Conservation Laws

*   **Week 11-13: Work and Energy**
*   **Week 14-16: Momentum and Impulse**
*   **Week 17-19: Circular Motion and Gravity**
*   **Week 20: Midterm Review & Exam**

### Quarter 3: Waves and Optics

*   **Week 21-23: Mechanical Waves and Sound**
*   **Week 24-26: Light, Reflection, and Mirrors**
*   **Week 27-29: Refraction and Lenses**
*   **Week 30: Quarter 3 Exam**

### Quarter 4: Electricity, Magnetism, and Modern Physics

*   **Week 31-33: Electrostatics**
*   **Week 34-36: Current Electricity (Circuits)**
*   **Week 37: Magnetism**
*   **Week 38: Modern Physics (Standard Model)**
*   **Week 39: Final Review**
*   **Week 40: Final Exam / Regents Exam**
